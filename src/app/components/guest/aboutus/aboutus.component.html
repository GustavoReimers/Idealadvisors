About Ideal Advisors

Ideal Advisors is a leading and prominent advisor owned financial services company. The ownership structure makes Ideal Advisors free of conflicts typically found in traditional brokerage firms and banks. Ideal Advisors is totally independent, yet fully supported by and connected to prominent financial partners in order to work with clients in the most possible efficient manner.

Built to Last

Ideal Advisors is fully capitalized by reputable private investors and senior financial services executives, who have shown their commitment and provided their resources despite challenging market conditions. Both the management team and advisors hold significant equity stakes in the company, making their long term success aligned with that of the company.

We were able to spot the growing crisis of confidence among investors well before the current financial crisis. Ideal Advisors was our answer; an alternative built on a very simple and clear idea: doing what is best for investors, 100% of the time.

Unconflicted Support

Our investors have no influence over our product selection or research, which is an issue that presents conflict of interest in traditional firms. The clients share our principals on the reliance of independent investment advice.

Experienced Management

Ideal Advisors evolved over considerable time, not overnight. Our management team shares the vision to create the best possible financial services model to suit the current times and market conditions. Each employee of the firm has had long career spans, having experienced numerous market cycles. Ideal Advisors approach to changing the paradigm for financial advice and advisors aims to create a stable business aligned with the need of our clients.

Our clients are provided with complete and sound advice as we believe firmly that we are the owners of a true partnership. As fiduciaries, the interest of our clients comes first.

Hauke Dinklage, Director
