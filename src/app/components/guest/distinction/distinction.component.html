We only have to look at newspapers and other media channels to get an idea. A critic may add that the notion of traditional banking (especially in the area of guidance) needs some serious work

We were able to spot the growing crisis of confidence among investors well before the current financial crisis. Ideal Advisors was our answer; an alternative built on a very simple and clear idea: doing what is best for investors, 100% of the time.
